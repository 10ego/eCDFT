Canadian Drug Facts Table - Head and Shoulders Anti-Dandruff Shampoo

Active ingredient

Pyrithione Zinc 30% w/w

Purpose

Dandruff control

Uses

relieves and/or reduces the recurrence of dandruff and/or symptoms of dandruff:
  • itching
  • irritation
  • redness
  • flaking
  • scaling

Warnings

For external use only.

Do not use on children under 2 years of age, unless directed by a doctor

When using this product avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse with water.

Stop use and ask a doctor if condition worsens or does not improve after regular use of this product as directed.

Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, call a poison control centre or get medical help right away.

Directions

Adults and children 2 years and older:

For shampoos and rinses

  • apply evenly to scalp, leave on for several minutes, and then rinse off (optional: repeat application)
  • use at least twice per week or as directed by a doctor or pharmacist

For leave on products

  • apply to scalp one to four times daily or as directed by a doctor or pharmacist

Inactive Ingredients

water/eau, sodium laureth sulfate, cocamide mea, cocamidopropyl betaine, acrylates copolymer, sodium chloride, parfum/fragrance, polyglyceryl-3 laurate, guar hydroxypropyltrimonium chloride, PEG-12 dimethicone, quaternium-80, disodium EDTA, sodium polynaphthalenesulfonate, SD alcohol 40-B, cellulose gum, PPG-12-buteth-16, elaeis guineensis (palm) oil, methylisothiazolinone, methylchloroisothiazolinone, green 5/CL 61570, red 33/CL-17200
