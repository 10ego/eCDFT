Canadian Drug Facts Table - Strepsils

Active ingredient

Amylmetacresol 0.06 mg

Purpose

Antiseptic Throat Lozenge

Uses

  • temporarily relieves sore throat
  • temporarily relieves discomfort of mouth and throat infections (for products containing amylmetacresol and dichlorobenzyl alcohol)

Warnings

Do not use

  • if you are allergic to any ingredients in the product.

Stop use and ask a doctor if

  • symptoms are severe or persist for more than 2 days
  • you notice any unusual symptoms

If pregnant or breastfeeding, ask a healthcare professional before use.

Keep out of reach of children. In case of overdose, call a poison control centre or get medical help right away, even if you do not notice any signs or symptoms.

Directions

Adults and children 6 years and older (for products containing amylmetacresol or dichlorobenzyl alcohol):
Adults and children 2 years and over:

  • dissolve one lozenge slowly in the mouth every 2-3 hours as needed
  • do not take more than 10 lozenges in 24 hours
