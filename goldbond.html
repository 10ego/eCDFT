Canadian Drug Facts Table - Gold Bond

Active ingredient

White petrolatum 30% w/w

Purpose

Skin Protectant

Uses

  • Temporarily protects and helps relieve minor skin irritation and itching of minor cuts, scrapes and burns

Warnings

For external use only.

Do not use

  • on broken skin (for powdered products)
  • on deep or puncture wounds, animal bites or serious burns (for products with mineral oil, petrolatum and white petrolatum)

When using this product avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse with water

Stop use and ask a doctor if symptoms worsen or last for more than 7 days

Other warning

Keep powder away from face to avoid inhalation, which can cause breathing problems (for powdered products)

Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, call a poison control centre or get medical help right away.

Directions

  • apply to affected area as needed

Aqua, glycerin, glyceryl stearate, dimethicone, behentrimonium methosulfate, cetyl alcohol, butylene glycol, distearyldimonium chloride, cetearyl alcohol, polysorbate 60, stearamidopropyl PG-dimonium chloride phosphate, propylene glycol, aloe barbadensis leaf juice, polyethylene, stearyl alcohol, methyl gluceth 20, steareth-2, steareth-21, DMDM hydantoin, jojoba esters, allantoin, hydrolyzed jojoba esters, methylparaben, tocopheryl acetate, C12-15 alkyl benzoate, tribehenin, ceramide-2, PEG-10 phytosterol, palmitoyl oligopeptide, cocodimonium hydroxypropyl hydrolyzed rice protein, hydrolyzed collagen, propylparaben, butyrospermum parkii (shea) butter extract, EDTA, potassium hydroxide, magnesium ascorbyl phosphate, retinyl palmitate.
